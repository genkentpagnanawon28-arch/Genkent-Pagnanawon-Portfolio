About Us

Serving Cebu Pet Lovers with Passion

Aquatierra Aquariums & Pets is your local destination for everything aquatic and furry. Located in the heart of Guadalupe, Cebu City, we've been helping hobbyists and pet parents create thriving underwater ecosystems and happy homes for their companions.

Whether you're setting up your first betta tank or maintaining a complex reef aquarium, our team provides trusted advice and quality supplies to ensure your success.
