Our Story

Crafting Beauty,
One Bouquet at a Time

For over 15 years, Glynson's has been Cebu's trusted destination for luxury floral artistry. What began as a small family passion has blossomed into the island's premier floral boutique.

We believe that flowers are more than decorations—they are emotions made visible. Each arrangement that leaves our studio is meticulously crafted by our master florists, combining traditional techniques with contemporary design sensibilities.

Our commitment to excellence means sourcing only the freshest premium blooms, from local Cebu farms to imported Ecuadorian roses, ensuring every petal is perfect for your special moments.
